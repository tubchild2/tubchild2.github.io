The Wolves of Arcohaem

The Wolves of Arcohaem are an elite Republic strike team deemed too unpredictable to be trusted in the formal military. They were originally a group of 8 Republic Majors, but a handful of their members have been lost. Vaesha died due to the premature detonation of one of Aeroneiros' devices. They didn't get along, so it's pretty suspicious, but it's unlikely that he actually did it on purpose. You never know though. Arrius sacrificed himself to save a Harengon family, and Rikkix vanished in a blizzard and has yet to be found.

Malgruff the Soul-Tearer

Species: Awakened Owlbear

Class: Rogue

Background: Fisher


Personality:
Malgruff is first and foremost a fisherman, and a wrathful one at that. He's clumsy, he lacks hierarchy, and he really hates elves (knife ears). He likes fish and rodents, and he has an enchanted fishing rod. His primary goal in life is to fish, and eat people who stop him from fishing. He also values doing the right thing (generally) but that moral compass only goes so far when people disturb his life's work.


Backstory:
Malgruff has a tragic backstory. He was adopted from an animal shelter as a young lad by a family of nobles. They were corrupt, and got angry at him. That much he could handle, it wasn't that bad. However, one fateful day they interrupted his fishing, and that was too far. He killed them all and joined the military soon after.

Aeroneiros

Species: Half-Elf

Class: Artificer

Background: Artisan



Personality:
Aeroneiros is an intelligent, creative, and largely absent-minded individual. He's obsessed with E-Tech, and with his inventions. He likes it when people appreciate his inventions, populated cities, justice, frostberries, and explosions. He doesn't like anything that's broken, farms, icetail eel, and people touching his blueprints. He's a little vindictive, arrogant, and impatient when it comes to dealing with stupid people. Also, his inventions have a bad history of going wrong (he doesn't like to talk about what happened with Vaesha). His primary goal is to complete his service to the Republic so that he can make his dream invention. He never wanted to join the military, but he was pressured by his parents, and he also realized how shockingly well it paid. If he completed his service, he'd be able to invent for the rest of his life! Also, he's got a custom toolkit that he can use to invent pretty much whatever he wants.



Backstory:
Aeroneiros was born in middle-class Skaad to two merchants. His inventions and contraptions would often malfunction, causing him to be the victim of much bullying and derision. He was even bullied by his parents who constantly nagged him to pursue a career in the Republic.

Doornail "The Bulwark" Stein

Species: Goliath (Stone Giant)

Class: Fighter - Paladin

Background: Sailor



Personality:
A lighthearted, direct, and protective individual. He uses simplistic language, has a heavy laugh, and pretends to be a philosopher. He likes meat stews, doors, the calm before the storm, and the thickest part of the storm / battle. He dislikes being hurt, and E-Tech. He's a practical fellow, so he'll still use E-Tech when it comes down to it, but he prefers classical combat. He would rather die than let someone else die, he has no sense of self preservation or fear of danger, he takes things literally, and another thing I see here in my notes that I don't want to put on the internet. His primary goal is to protect people, and to protect his way of life.



Backstory:
He lived in a southern village named Hak'Thor in Freyland. He was a little guy, and a late bloomer. His dad was Doorhinge, and his mother was named Nailstrike. They were a loving family. One night, a boat docked on the shores with no one on board. At least, they thought no one was on board. Something made its way into the village, and in one night, took out half of the town. It looked like a shadow with red eyes, horns, and large claws. It hurt to look at, like the light was vanishing around it. Doornail barely survived the encounter with what has now been colloquially deemed "the Wraith" by local drunks and conspiracy theorists. His inability to save his parents and the image of that thing will haunt him for the rest of his life. You know what else will haunt him for the rest of his life? The fact that he's one of the only people to ever actually see the Wraith and live and no one will ever believe him.

Once he grew, he took to the Depths Frigid for blubber harvesting and deep net fishing. He yearns for the sea, as the children yearn for the mines. He's a traditionalist at heart, being from the south and all, but not a radicalized and violent one like the Frigoshians. Unity and peace is more important to him than pure history and culture, so he sided with the Republic.

Brogus Bobbleheim

Species: Orc

Class: Monk / Rogue

Background: Soldier



Personality:
Brogus is a very kind individual for someone of his size. After years of PMC work, he's now just content with reading, sipping tea, and dumping. Sometimes all at once. He loves chilling in a cave by a fire with his friends, and is essentially a dirty red coat in disguise. He dislikes conflict and values people's lives, so he will always try to find a better way. Unfortunately, in his line of work, most people don't listen to reason. He's unable to admit when he's wrong, and will usually blame other people. He hates that he's a skilled hand to hand fighter, and apologizes the whole time. His primary goal in life as of now is to retire. He wants this to be his final job. He years for the finer things in life, and hopes he'll be able to sit back and relax in his sunset years (even though he's only in his 40's). As long as he can get the job done, and help people along the way, he'll be happy. But, if he can prevent any conflict along the way, he'll be sure to give it a shot.



Backstory:
Raised in Lemnaeus by a pretty wealthy family of exporters. His childhood wasn't really that interesting. He didn't go into the family business, but nowadays, he wishes he had. Years and years of being a combat specialist has taken its toll, and he's got the scars to prove it. His family is native to Lemnaeus, but they usually spend most of their time in and around the Great Saline Sea.
